Practical Safety Tips

  • Trust your instincts: If a situation feels unsafe, remove yourself when possible.
  • Share your location: Let a trusted person know your route or use location-sharing features.
  • Plan exits: On arrival to places, note exits and well-lit areas.
  • Carry emergency essentials: Phone, charged power bank, small flashlight, whistle, or self-defense tool where legal.
  • Stay aware: Avoid distractions like prolonged headphone use in unfamiliar areas.
